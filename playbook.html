33%

33%

This structure is guidance, not a requirement. Traders with smaller accounts may enter the full position at once.
OBJECTIVE

SECTION 05
05

Signal Lifecycle

Signals are not static alerts. They evolve through a defined lifecycle from issuance to resolution.

SIGNAL ISSUED
Oracle publishes the setup with all parameters defined. Initial 33% entry begins.
TRIGGER CONFIRMED
Price crosses the trigger level. The market is confirming the thesis. Second 33% allocation added.
POSITION BUILT
Full position established on the first pullback to trigger. Trade is now 100% active.
TRADE DEVELOPS
Price moves toward targets. Confidence score updates in real time. T1, T2, T3 tracked live.
RESOLUTION
Trade reaches target, hits invalidation, or is closed early. Result recorded permanently.
WHY THIS MATTERS
TRADES CAN STAY OPEN FOR WEEKS
Some setups take time to develop. Patience is part of the system.
SOME TRADES CLOSE EARLY
Risk management may require exiting before target is reached.
SOME SIGNALS NEVER TRIGGER
If the market doesn't confirm, you remain in the initial 33% position only. The remaining 67% is never deployed.
