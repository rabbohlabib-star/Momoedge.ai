What Oracle Is

Oracle is a decision-support terminal that combines structured technical analysis with AI-assisted probability assessment to produce fully defined trade setups.

🔍
IDENTIFIES OPPORTUNITIES
Structured trading opportunities detected through systematic market analysis.
📐
PREDEFINED FRAMEWORKS
Every signal includes entry, invalidation, targets, and execution parameters.
📡
REAL-TIME TRACKING
Trades tracked live from issuance through resolution. Nothing disappears.

ORACLE DOES NOT

Trade Execution Framework

Now that you understand the terminal, here's how to execute. Positions are built progressively as the market confirms the thesis.

33%
INITIAL ENTRY
Entered immediately when the signal is issued.
33%
CONFIRMATION ENTRY
Added once price crosses the trigger level.
33%
PULLBACK ENTRY
Final allocation on first retest of the trigger.
This structure is guidance, not a requirement. Traders with smaller accounts may enter full position at once.
SIGNAL LIFECYCLE
SIGNAL ISSUED
Oracle publishes the setup. Initial 33% entry begins.
TRIGGER CONFIRMED
Price crosses trigger. Second 33% added.
POSITION BUILT
Full position on first pullback to trigger.
MINIMUM HOLD PERIOD
No early exits until hold time elapses. Only invalidation overrides.
TRADE DEVELOPS
Price moves toward targets. Confidence updates live.
RESOLUTION
Target hit, invalidation, or closed early after min hold. Recorded permanently.
TRADES CAN STAY OPEN FOR WEEKS
Some setups take time. Patience is part of the system.
MIN HOLD TIME IS ENFORCED
Every signal has a min hold period. No early exits until it elapses — only invalidation overrides.
SOME SIGNALS NEVER TRIGGER
If the market doesn't confirm, you stay in the initial 33% only. The remaining 67% is never deployed.
