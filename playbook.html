Trade Execution Framework

Positions are built progressively as the market confirms the thesis. Capital is not committed all at once.

33%
INITIAL ENTRY
Entered immediately when the signal is issued.
33%
CONFIRMATION ENTRY
Added once price crosses the trigger level.
33%
PULLBACK ENTRY
Final allocation on the first retest of the trigger.
This structure is guidance, not a requirement. Traders with smaller accounts may enter the full position at once.
OBJECTIVE
Build the position as the market proves the thesis correct, rather than committing full capital upfront.

SECTION 05
05

Signal Lifecycle

Signals are not static alerts. They evolve through a defined lifecycle from issuance to resolution.

SIGNAL ISSUED
Oracle publishes the setup with all parameters defined. Initial 33% entry begins.
TRIGGER CONFIRMED
Price crosses the trigger level. The market is confirming the thesis. Second 33% allocation added.
POSITION BUILT
Full position established on the first pullback to trigger. Trade is now 100% active.
MINIMUM HOLD PERIOD
Every signal has a minimum hold time (e.g. 30 days). No early exits are permitted before this period elapses — the thesis needs time to develop. Only invalidation overrides this rule.
TRADE DEVELOPS
Price moves toward targets. Confidence score updates in real time. T1, T2, T3 tracked live.
RESOLUTION
Trade reaches target, hits invalidation, or is closed early after the minimum hold period. Result recorded permanently.
WHY THIS MATTERS
TRADES CAN STAY OPEN FOR WEEKS
Some setups take time to develop. Patience is part of the system.
MINIMUM HOLD TIME IS ENFORCED
Every signal has a minimum hold period. No early exits are permitted until this time elapses — only invalidation overrides this rule.
SOME TRADES CLOSE EARLY
After the minimum hold period, risk management may require exiting before target is reached.
SOME SIGNALS NEVER TRIGGER
If the market doesn't confirm, you remain in the initial 33% position only. The remaining 67% is never deployed.
